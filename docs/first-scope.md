# Pilot Scope Suggestion

Department: Rotating equipment maintenance (compressors + pumps)

Why:
- Clear recurring failure patterns
- Strong tacit troubleshooting dependence
- High asset uptime impact
- Easy to define asset-task-symptom taxonomy

Success metric for 8-week pilot:
- 30+ approved records
- 70% of recurring faults mapped to at least one approved method
- 20+ shadowing records
- Median search-to-answer time under 60 seconds
