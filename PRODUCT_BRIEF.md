\f0\fs24 \cf0 # PRODUCT BRIEF\
\
## Working name\
Knowledge Continuity Platform\
\
## One-line summary\
A mobile-first app for engineering firms to capture, validate, retrieve, and transfer technician know-how before it is lost to retirement.\
\
## Problem\
Critical engineering knowledge often lives in the heads of senior technicians. When they retire or leave, firms lose troubleshooting logic, field fixes, task judgement, and practical shortcuts that are hard to replace.\
\
## Users\
- Technicians\
- Senior technicians / subject matter experts\
- Supervisors\
- Reviewers / engineering managers\
- Administrators\
\
## Core use cases\
1. A technician records a field note with photos and symptom tags\
2. A senior expert records an expert interview answering structured prompts\
3. A junior technician logs a shadowing session with a senior colleague\
4. A supervisor reviews and approves a procedure or lesson learned\
5. A user searches by machine, system, task, or fault symptom\
6. Management sees which knowledge areas depend on one ageing expert\
\
## MVP boundaries\
In scope:\
- Core records\
- Search\
- Review states\
- Dashboards\
- Seed data\
- Mobile-friendly capture flows\
\
Out of scope for v1:\
- Full offline sync\
- AI transcription production integration\
- Enterprise SSO\
- Advanced analytics\
- Multi-tenant architecture unless trivial to support cleanly\
\
## Core taxonomy\
- Asset / machine\
- System / subsystem\
- Task\
- Symptom / fault\
- Environment / site\
- Skill level\
- Approval state\
\
## Success criteria\
- A user can create and find knowledge quickly\
- A reviewer can approve content\
- A junior can browse approved methods and field notes\
- The dashboard clearly shows knowledge-risk hotspots}
